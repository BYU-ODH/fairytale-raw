About

Description

Associated with storytelling at the hearth, bedtime stories around an illustrated book, and more frequently with
Disney movies and contemporary television dramas, fairy tales must be (re)considered in parallel with successive
breakthroughs in communicative media. For example, although fairy tale film is similar to fairy tales on television,
the former historically has been more public and the latter more domestic.

Data Visualization

This project reassembles bibliographic information about fairy tale presence in American television shows in
order to create visual representations of the data and more nuanced interpretations or understanding of their
place. Some of our goals include tracking specific tales, broadcast information, tele vision genre, time lines, and
the influence of Disney.
